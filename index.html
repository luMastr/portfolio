D4T - Exposing the truth of clothing

Problem

Explore new sustainable opportunities in existing apps of E-commerce or Travel and integrate one or more of following design challenge:

  • - Make users aware of their carbon footprint
  • - Help users reduce their carbon footprint
  • - Include circular economy practices.

Solution

D4T supports users wanting to make eco-friendly purchases in fashion. During the moment of purchase, D4T helps users understand the impact of a fashion item in terms of Water usage, CO2 and microplastics production. The core principle of D4T revolves around swiftly and efficiently acquiring essential information.

Ease - Cultivating a Positive Mindset During Menopause

Problem

Explore innovative uses of intelligent voice agents to enhance personal health literacy and awareness. Develop voice-based interfaces with complementary channels to help users manage intimate well-being at home. Focus on:

  • - Antibiotics usage and AMR (Antimicrobial Resistance) awareness
  • - Knowledge about UTIs (cystitis)
  • - Intimate health education
  • - Women's health

Solution

Ease offers an ecosystem that supports women's mental health and knowledge during menopause. It provides personalized guidance through user conversations, access to medical content to bridge knowledge gaps, and a community for sharing tips and experiences. Ease also offers specific exercises to address physical and psychological symptoms, helping women transition from dysfunctional to functional behaviors.

Infinite Strolling - Rethinking limitless content consumption

Problem

Crafting an installation that delves into contemporary perspectives on photography within the digital era.

Solution

Infinite Strolling is an interactive installation that encourages participants to rethink limitless image consumption through mindless scrolling, especially within platforms like TikTok. The installation features treadmills that regulate the scrolling speed of a TikTok feed displayed on a screen. Viewers have the option to observe passively or actively engage by stepping onto the treadmills to control the scrolling speed, enhancing the interactive experience.

Pyxis - Find your constellation

Problem

The project involved exploring potential applications using Arduino components within a compact 15x15cm enclosure. The objective was to investigate innovative electronic solutions and develop prototypes that leverage Arduino technology in space-constrained environments.

Solution

Pyxis is an enigmatic black box designed to redefine the way we discover and appreciate constellations in the night sky. Incorporating an internal compass, Pyxis empowers users to embark on a guided celestial journey, effortlessly navigating the cosmos with precision and wonder. Through a curated list of visible constellations, users interface with Pyxis to select their desired constellations. Leveraging its internal compass, Pyxis dynamically orients itself, providing intuitive directional guidance to aid users in locating the chosen constellation amidst the vast expanse of stars in the sky.

MC - A visual representation of morse code

Problem

The project involved exploring potential applications using a 32x32 LED matrix. The objective was to investigate how this technology could be creatively utilized to develop prototypes and innovative solutions across various domains.

Solution

MC represents an interactive and artistic installation that utilizes a 32x32 LED matrix to bring to light the most famous messages in the history of humanity, expressed through the universal language of Morse code. The main objective is to create an engaging experience where history and technology converge into a captivating visual effect.

RadioCast - An interactive podcast player

Problem

Re-fabricate an iconic product by upgrading its function for a more contemporary use.

  • - The object should be large enough to hold a smartphone and the electronic board with the buttons/potentiometers/sensors.
  • - The UI should be built using Figma with different frames that can be selected using the keyboard keys.
  • - Design it in Fusion 360 with laser-cutting and 3D printing in mind.

Solution

RadioCast is an interactive podcast player. Listen to what you like with different modes: one allows you to filter the podcast selection, and the other allows you to play a podcast randomly. If you enjoy sports or food, for example, a whole selection of podcasts is already planned for you! You can switch modes at any time, with a single movement. Enhance your car trips or make the most of your sunbathing time at the beach. Explore the world with RadioCast!
